Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is one of germans favorite dish. It's similar to spaghetti but tastes much better. My lasagna is full of flavour and should be tried by everybody.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage
  • 3/pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • fresh parsley
  • little bit of salt
  • italian seasoning
  • fennel seeds
  • ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozarella cheese
  • 3/4 cups grated parmesan

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
